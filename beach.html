Riki Tiki Beach

Immerse yourself in the vibrant beach experience at Riki Tiki Beach Resort, where the golden sands meet the azure waters of the ocean. Enjoy the sun-drenched shores with amenities like volleyball courts, inviting you to join in friendly matches or simply soak in the lively atmosphere. For those seeking relaxation, indulge in our courtesy beach lay down chairs and umbrellas, providing a comfortable spot to unwind and soak up the tropical ambiance. Whether you're basking in the sun, enjoying beach activities, or simply watching the waves roll in, the beach at Riki Tiki Beach Resort offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation for your tropical getaway.

Activities

Experience a world of adventure and relaxation on the pristine shores of Riki Tiki Beach Resort. Start your day with a peaceful beach yoga session, held daily at sunrise and sunset, offering a rejuvenating experience for mind and body. As the day unfolds, immerse yourself in the crystal-clear waters with a snorkeling excursion, guided by our experienced instructors. For the thrill-seekers, surfing lessons are available, with expert surf coaches ensuring a safe and exhilarating experience. After an active day, unwind on the beach with our courtesy beach lay down chairs and umbrellas, available for rent at affordable prices. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or simply looking to soak up the sun, Riki Tiki Beach Resort offers an array of beach activities to suit every taste and budget.

About Us
