About Us

Our History

Explore the rich history of Riki Tiki Beach Resort, from its humble beginnings as a small beachfront retreat to its current status as a premier destination for travelers from around the world. Founded in 1984, our resort has been a beacon of hospitality and relaxation, offering guests a unique blend of luxury and comfort in a tropical paradise.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities and services to meet the evolving needs of our guests, while remaining true to our core values of providing exceptional service, creating unforgettable experiences, and preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings.

Meet Our Team

At Riki Tiki Beach Resort, our team is the heart and soul of our operation. Comprised of dedicated professionals with a passion for hospitality, our team works tirelessly to ensure that every guest has a memorable and enjoyable stay with us.

From our experienced chefs who create delectable dishes using locally sourced ingredients, to our friendly front desk staff who are always ready to assist with any request, each member of our team plays a vital role in creating the welcoming and relaxing atmosphere that defines the Riki Tiki experience.
