Amenities

Nestled along the sun-kissed shores of our tropical oasis, Riki Tiki Beach Resort offers a delightful array of amenities to enhance your stay. Dive into relaxation at our sparkling pool, where the cool waters offer a refreshing escape from the sun-drenched days. Unwind under the starlit sky at our inviting fire pits, where the warm glow creates a perfect ambiance for gathering with friends and family. For a soothing retreat, indulge in our rejuvenating hot tub, the ideal spot to unwind after a day of adventure. Experience the vibrant flavors of the tropics at our tiki bar, where exotic cocktails and island-inspired cuisine await. Venture out to our serene lake, where you can paddle or simply soak in the peaceful surroundings. For some friendly competition, challenge your companions to a game of cornhole, adding a fun and lively touch to your stay. At Riki Tiki Beach Resort, every moment is crafted to offer you a truly unforgettable tropical experience.
