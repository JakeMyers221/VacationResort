Welcome to Riki Tiki Resort!

Welcome to Riki Tiki Beach Resort, a tropical oasis nestled along the sun-kissed shores of paradise. Our resort offers a blend of relaxation and adventure, where guests can unwind amidst breathtaking ocean views and lush palm trees. Dive into our sparkling pool or rejuvenate in our soothing hot tub, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring. Experience the warmth of our fire pits as you gather under the starlit sky, creating memories with friends and family. Step onto our pristine beach, where golden sands meet crystal-clear waters, inviting you to bask in the sun or take a refreshing dip. Indulge in tropical delights at our vibrant tiki bar, where exotic cocktails and island-inspired cuisine await. At Riki Tiki Beach Resort, every moment is an opportunity to embrace the beauty of the tropics and create unforgettable experiences.

Location

Located on the stunning shores of Destin Florida, Riki Tiki Beach Resort offers a captivating coastal retreat. The resort's pristine beaches boast soft golden sands that gently meet the crystal-clear waters of the ocean, creating a picturesque setting for relaxation and adventure. Guests can enjoy the tranquil ambiance, whether lounging under the sun or exploring the underwater world through snorkeling or diving. The gentle waves provide a soothing soundtrack, perfect for unwinding and embracing the natural beauty of the surroundings. With its breathtaking views and serene atmosphere, Riki Tiki Beach Resort promises an unforgettable tropical getaway.
